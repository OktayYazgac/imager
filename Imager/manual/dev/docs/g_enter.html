Starting from Futuris Imager 2.01 (and later versions), I offer several commercial services, that may be useful to you and your company:
Enterprise customization
Custom plug-ins development
FIPIS plug-ins usage licenses
Partial source sales

Enterprise customization makes it possible for organizations and individuals to brand Futuris Imager.

Features:

  • installer customization (additional licenses and documents displayed during installation, new bitmaps and signs, additional (or removed) components, silent installations)
  • custom toolbar icons, skins, menu items (may link to your sites)
  • custom (additional) help topics
  • and much more...

With custom plug-ins development you can order custom plug-in for Futuris Imager (for example it is the best way to introduce new graphical format to the world):

Features:

  • modifications (and possible branding) of existing plug-ins
  • creation of new plug-ins of all types supported by Futuris Imager
  • support and redistribution of new plug-ins

FIPIS plug-ins usage licenses includes:

  • licenses for commercial use of Futuris Imager plug-ins with your applications (Imager itself is free for commercial use)
  • free licenses for non-commercial use of Futuris Imager plug-ins with your application (however, in this case your app shouldn't compete with Imager in any way)

Partial source sales - you can buy parts of Futuris Imager source for use inside your own application.

 

Prices for all above items depend on number of potencial users of modified software or amount of work or amount of code you wish to buy, etc - so, if you are interested - please, contact me.
