FuturixImager System Requirements
 

Basic requirements:

  • Windows XP, Windows 2000, or Windows 2003 Server
    FuturixImager also works on Windows XP x64 (as 32-bit application)
  • At least 16mb of free RAM (35 to 60mb for huge images)
  • At least 6mb of free space on hard drive
  • 15-bit or better display mode (32-bit mode recommended)

 

Requirements for specific components:

  • If you want to scan from Imager, your scanner should support WIA or TWAIN standards (99% of all scanners do)
