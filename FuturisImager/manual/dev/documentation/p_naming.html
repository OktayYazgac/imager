Naming Convernsions
Back to index
 

There are simple rules to name your plug-ins:

  1. Your plug-ins shouldn't start with "f_" (standard Futuris plug-ins use it).
  2. Your plug-in shouldn't contain "futuris" or "futuris_" (used by additional Futuris plug-ins).
  3. You can use subfolders to store your plug-ins (create your own special folder name, that cannot by used by someome else - your name or company name for example).
